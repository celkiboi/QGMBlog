About Quick Garage Manager Blog

The quick garage manager blog was founded by QuickSoftware d.d. in Osijek, Croatia. With a mission to bring information, reviews, and discussion of high-end cars closer to the consumer. The creation of the blog began in December 2023, with its operation starting in January 2024.

About QuickSoftware d.d.

QuickSoftware d.d. is an organization that specializes in delivering highly optimized software solutions that span multiple fields. We offer solutions in web development, cross-platform app development, requirement engineering, embedded software, Internet of Things (IoT), blockchain, machine learning (ML), Artificial Intelligence (AI), big data, digital marketing, game development, testing, certifications of software and hardware, as well as many more services.

About our mission

Our mission of providing information that will bridge the gap between the consumer and high-end cars is not an easy one. With more and more high-end car manufacturers, and more and more different products with more and more options, it is imperative that a high-quality blog keeps the consumer informed. Our team of expert engineers, racing drivers, and journalists are well-armed going into this battle.
